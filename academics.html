Academic & Productivity Resources

Study Architecture

  • The Pomodoro Framework

    Enforce strict 25-minute periods of deep work separated by 5-minute tactical breaks. This limits cognitive fatigue.

  • Active Recall Iteration

    Halt passive reading. Continuously formulate and answer questions regarding the material to simulate testing environments.

High-Stakes Examination Relief

  • 4-7-8 Breathing

    A biological override for the nervous system: 4-second inhale, 7-second hold, 8-second exhale. Use immediately prior to exams.

  • Circadian Defense

    Prioritize 8 hours of sleep cycle completion before an exam. The memory retention capabilities of sleep vastly outweigh cramming.
